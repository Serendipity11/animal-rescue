Dog adoptions save lives. Adopt a dog and you'll have a friend for life!

How to Adopt:

First Step: Submit Your Application.

Download the application below and send it in with the name of whomever you are interested in as the subject of an email.

If you are interested in adopting any of our awesome rescues please follow the steps and follow directions to ensure that your application gets the consideration it deserves.

Second Step: Application Review & Approval

The application provides important information. We will be able to determine if the adoption is in the pet's best interest, and the process ensures that you will find a pet well suited to your lifestyle.

If your application is approved, an email will be sent with follow-up instructions.

Adoption Fees: Our adoption fees are comparable to those found amongst other well-known rescues.
