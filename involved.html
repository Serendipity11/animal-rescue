Volunteering:

We are always looking for volunteers to help out at events and take care of our awesome rescues! This is a great way for students to get in community service hours!

This Includes:

  • one time volunteers opportunities such as transports, adoption events, and supply pickups.
  • long term opportunities such as application processing including reference checks and spay/neuter clinics.

Donating:

If you can't foster or volunteer, donations are always a great way to make an impact! Donations are accepted in the form of money or physical items - anything is appreciated. PayPal donations make what we do possible.

Donate VIA:

  • PayPal: TFPRM
  • Venmo: seniordogs-venice

We accept donations of supplies such as pee pads, dog beds, collars, leashes, harnesses, dog bowls, towels,small blankets,dog food (dry & canned), medicane, vitamins and more. Donations are also accepted via our Amazon Wishlist (link below).

You can drop off at our vets office: Jacaranda animal hospital 725 Shamrock blvd Venice, FL 34293
